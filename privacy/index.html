Vigil — Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-07-16
Contact: support@getvigil.app

Vigil is a scam-detection app for iPhone. This policy explains what Vigil does with your data in plain language.

WHO RUNS VIGIL
Vigil is published by Yariv T. We are an independent developer. No advertising, no data brokers, no data resale.

WHAT VIGIL IS
Vigil analyzes content you explicitly share with it:
• Scanner / Message check — text, screenshots, or video you paste or share
• Profile photos — dating or social pictures you choose to check
• Video check — screen recordings, call clips, or live camera frames you start (e.g. watching another device)
• Live calls — microphone audio you start and stop manually
• Audio check — voicemail or voice-note files you pick or share
• Dating coach — chat threads or screenshots you paste for coaching insights
• Vault — a local on-device history of past analyses

Optional system features (when enabled in iOS Settings):
• Message Filter — on-device heuristics for unknown SMS/MMS (not iMessage). Message bodies stay on your phone.
• Call Directory / Call Shield — curated scam phone numbers for caller ID labels. Numbers come from Vigil’s public threat-intel lists, not your contacts.

Vigil does not read your texts, emails, or photo library in the background.

WHAT LEAVES YOUR PHONE
Some analysis uses cloud models. What may be sent when you use that feature:

• Scanner text — the text you pasted
• Scanner images — the image(s) you attached
• Scanner / Video check video — the clip you attached (scam or deepfake analysis)
• Live video watch — short JPEG frames from your camera while you keep Live watch running
• Audio check — transcript and analysis features (raw audio is processed on-device when models are present; cloud may receive transcript/features)
• Live calls — on-device speech transcripts and, when cloud assist is active, short audio clips (WAV) for scam-language analysis while a session is running
• Dating coach — pasted chat text and attached screenshots or video you add
• Ask Vigil / Consult — the verdict and message context for follow-up questions

Optional (Profile toggles):
• Share crash reports (Sentry) — anonymous crash diagnostics (stack traces). Off by default.
• Share verdict feedback — anonymous thumbs, risk band, scam-category labels, app/TI version, and a random install id. When readable scan text exists, a short redacted snippet is included automatically on both thumbs up and thumbs down (phones, emails, and links removed; truncated). Turn the toggle off to stop uploads.

Threat-intelligence updates (OTA): Vigil may download curated public scam lists (phrases, reported numbers/domains, playbooks) over HTTPS. Those bundles contain no content from your device.

We do not send: your full contacts list, your location for scam detection, ad identifiers, or your full photo library beyond what you choose to analyze.

THIRD PARTIES
• Google Gemini — analysis of content you share (see Google API Terms and Privacy Notice). Vigil uses paid API access.
• xAI Grok (optional) — only when configured in the build, for select second-opinion checks (e.g. links, video, profile photos, live frames).
• Sentry (optional, opt-in) — crash diagnostics if you turn on “Share crash reports”.
• Vigil feedback relay (optional, opt-in) — thin relay that stores anonymous feedback events for product tuning (not an advertising network).
• Apple TestFlight / App Store — standard crash and install metrics during beta / distribution.

WHAT WE STORE
On your device: Vault history, Safety Anchor contact, preferences, dating match data, share inbox, local feedback events.
On Vigil-operated storage: we do not run a general content backend for your scans. Opt-in anonymous feedback events may be stored via the feedback relay for tuning. Cloud providers process analysis requests under their terms; Vigil does not keep a separate copy of your prompts beyond what those services retain.

PERMISSIONS
• Microphone — Live calls only while you tap Start listening. Short clips may be sent to Gemini when cloud assist is on.
• Speech recognition — on-device captions during Live calls
• Photo library — when you pick images or video to analyze
• Camera — capture / Live video watch / short clips for analysis
• Contacts — when you pick one Safety Anchor contact (local only; not uploaded)

YOUR RIGHTS
Your Vault data is on your phone — delete entries or uninstall to remove it. Turn off feedback/crash toggles to stop those uploads. Email support@getvigil.app with questions.

For users in the EU/UK (GDPR) and California (CCPA): processing of content you share is based on your consent to use Vigil for scam analysis. We do not sell personal information.

CHANGES
We update this policy when Vigil’s behavior changes. Material changes are noted in-app when practical. Hosted copy: temporary GitHub Pages URL until getvigil.app/privacy TLS is restored.

Plain summary: Vigil only analyzes what you share. History stays on your phone. Optional feedback may include a short redacted text snippet. We don’t sell your data.
